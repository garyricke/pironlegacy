Why This Scholarship

A decade of building entrepreneurs

Through the Batavia High School INCubator program, Dennis Piron guided countless students as they turned raw ideas into real businesses — coaching them through everything from pro-forma financials to pitch night in front of a room full of investors.

He taught them courage, teamwork, and what it feels like to bring something to life. This scholarship — awarded annually to a graduating senior who carries that spirit forward — makes his impact permanent.

"There's a tough love that prepares them in ways I don't know that college does. Nobody like him. Nobody." — Gary Ricke, Mentor
